# Age Verification Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2025

## 1. Minimum Age Requirement

Undercity is intended for users who are **18 years of age or older**.

This requirement exists because:
- The game contains simulated violence and crime themes
- The game contains simulated gambling features (Casino)
- The game contains mature thematic content

## 2. Age Gate

All new users must confirm they are 18+ before:
- Registering an account
- Accessing game content

## 3. Verification Method

We currently use **self-declaration** age verification:
- Users must confirm their date of birth during registration
- Users must check an "I am 18 or older" confirmation box
- This confirmation is stored with timestamp and IP

## 4. False Declaration

Users who falsely declare their age:
- Are in breach of our Terms of Service
- May have their account permanently terminated
- Parents/guardians who discover a minor has created an account
  should contact us immediately at katanas.reaper@gmail.com

## 5. COPPA Compliance (Under 13)

We do **NOT** knowingly collect data from children under 13.

If we discover a user is under 13:
- Their account will be immediately terminated
- All their data will be deleted within 30 days
- Parents can request data deletion at katanas.reaper@gmail.com

## 6. Parental Controls

If you are a parent and believe your child has created an account:
1. Email katanas.reaper@gmail.com immediately
2. Include: username or email used to register
3. We will verify and delete the account within 48 hours

## 7. GDPR / DPDP Act (India) — Children's Data

Under GDPR (EU), children under 16 require parental consent.
Under India's DPDP Act 2023, children under 18 require parental consent.

We set our minimum age at 18 to comply with the strictest applicable standard.

## 8. Future Verification

We plan to implement more robust age verification before public launch,
potentially including:
- Date of birth collection and validation
- Third-party age verification services
- Credit card verification (18+ proxy)
