# Gambling Disclaimer

**Last Updated:** June 2025

## 1. No Real Money Gambling

Undercity's Casino feature uses **virtual in-game currency only**.

- No real money is wagered
- No real money can be won
- Virtual currency has **no real-world monetary value**
- Virtual currency **cannot be exchanged** for real money
- Virtual currency **cannot be withdrawn** in any form

## 2. This Is Not Gambling

Under most jurisdictions, Undercity's Casino does not constitute
legal gambling because:
- No real money or items of real-world value are at stake
- Virtual currency cannot be converted to real money
- The game is for entertainment purposes only

## 3. Age Restriction

Despite not being real gambling, our Casino feature is restricted to
users **18 years of age or older** due to the simulated gambling themes.

These are **simulations for entertainment** within a fictional game world.
