# Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2025

## 1. Current Status — Free to Play

Undercity is currently **completely free to play**.
No purchases are required or available at this time.
Therefore, this refund policy is a forward-looking document
for when paid features are introduced.

## 2. Future Paid Features

If/when we introduce paid features, this policy will govern refunds.

## 3. General Refund Policy (Future)

### 3.1 Digital Goods
Virtual items and currency purchased with real money:
- **Within 14 days of purchase:** Full refund if item not used
- **After 14 days:** No refund (digital goods exemption)
- **Used items:** No refund

### 3.2 Subscriptions (If Introduced)
- Cancel anytime — no future charges
- Current billing period: no refund
- Annual plans: pro-rated refund within 14 days

### 3.3 Exceptions — Always Refunded
- Duplicate accidental purchases
- Technical failure preventing access to purchased content
- Service unavailability exceeding 72 consecutive hours

## 4. Account Bans

**No refunds** will be issued if your account is banned for:
- Violating Terms of Service
- Cheating or exploiting
- Any other policy violation

This applies to any real-money purchases made before the ban.
