## 3. Your Rights Under GDPR

### 3.1 Right of Access (Art. 15)
You can request a copy of all data we hold about you.
**How:** Email katanas.reaper@gmail.com with subject "Data Access Request"
**Response time:** Within 30 days

### 3.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16)
You can request correction of inaccurate data.
**How:** Email katanas.reaper@gmail.com with subject "Data Correction Request"

### 3.3 Right to Erasure / Right to be Forgotten (Art. 17)
You can request deletion of your account and all associated data.
**How:** Email katanas.reaper@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request"
**Note:** Some data may be retained for legal/security reasons (fraud prevention)

### 3.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 18)
You can request we limit how we use your data.
**How:** Email katanas.reaper@gmail.com with subject "Processing Restriction Request"

### 3.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)
You can request your data in a machine-readable format (JSON).
**How:** Email katanas.reaper@gmail.com with subject "Data Export Request"
**Response time:** Within 30 days

### 3.6 Right to Object (Art. 21)
You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.
**How:** Email katanas.reaper@gmail.com with subject "Processing Objection"

### 3.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Art. 22)
Our anti-cheat system makes automated decisions (bans, shadow bans).
You have the right to:
- Know when automated decisions affect you
- Request human review of automated decisions
- Challenge automated decisions

**How:** Email katanas.reaper@gmail.com with subject "Automated Decision Review"
