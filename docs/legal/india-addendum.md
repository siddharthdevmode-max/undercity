## 3. Lawful Basis for Processing

Under Section 4 and Section 7 of the DPDP Act, we process your personal data on the following lawful bases:

### 3.1 Consent (Section 6)
You provide explicit, informed, free, specific, and unambiguous consent at the time of account creation. You may withdraw consent at any time by:
- Deleting your account via `privacy@undercity.online`;
- Or using the self-service deletion endpoint when available.

Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before the withdrawal.

### 3.2 Legitimate Uses (Section 7)
We may process your data without explicit consent for:
- **Section 7(a)** — Voluntarily provided data for the purpose you provided it (e.g., support emails);
- **Section 7(b)** — Performance of a function under law;
- **Section 7(c)** — Compliance with a court order or judgment;
- **Section 7(e)** — Responding to medical emergencies (not applicable in normal gameplay);
- **Section 7(i)** — Employment-related purposes (not applicable to users).

We rely on **legitimate uses** for our anti-cheat system ("UAC"), security audit logs, and fraud prevention, as these are necessary to protect the integrity of the Service and the rights of other users.

## 4. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act

You, as a Data Principal under the DPDP Act, have the following rights:

### 4.1 Right to Access (Section 11)
You may request a summary of:
- The personal data we are processing about you;
- The activities for which we process such data;
- The identities of Data Fiduciaries and Data Processors with whom we have shared your data.

### 4.2 Right to Correction and Erasure (Section 12)
You may request:
- Correction of inaccurate or misleading personal data;
- Completion of incomplete personal data;
- Updating of outdated personal data;
- Erasure of personal data that is no longer necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, unless retention is required by law.

### 4.3 Right of Grievance Redressal (Section 13)
You may file a grievance with us before approaching the Data Protection Board of India. We will respond to grievances within **seven (7) working days**, as required by the DPDP Act.

To file a grievance, email **`privacy@undercity.online`** with the subject line `DPDP Grievance — [BRIEF DESCRIPTION]`.

### 4.4 Right to Nominate (Section 14)
You may nominate another individual to exercise your rights under the DPDP Act in the event of your death or incapacity. To do so, email `privacy@undercity.online` with:
- Your full name and registered email;
- The nominee's full name, contact information, and relationship to you;
- A signed (digital or scanned) statement of nomination.

### 4.5 Right to Withdraw Consent
At any time, with the same ease as it was given. Use the email above or the self-service deletion endpoint when available.

## 5. Your Duties Under the DPDP Act (Section 15)

The DPDP Act also places certain duties on you as a Data Principal:

- You must **not register false or frivolous grievances** with us or the Data Protection Board;
- You must **not furnish false particulars** or impersonate another person when providing your personal data;
- You must **not suppress material information** when providing personal data;
- You must **comply with the provisions of applicable law** when exercising your rights.

Violation of these duties may result in penalties under Section 33 of the DPDP Act (up to ₹10,000 per instance).

## 6. Children's Data (Section 9)

The Service is **strictly for users aged 18 and above**. We do not knowingly collect personal data from any individual under 18.

Under Section 9 of the DPDP Act, processing personal data of a child (defined as anyone under 18) requires **verifiable parental consent**. Because we operate an 18+ service, we instead **prohibit registration by anyone under 18** and will permanently delete any account found to belong to an underage user.

We do **NOT** undertake:
- Tracking or behavioral monitoring of children;
- Targeted advertising directed at children;
- Any processing likely to cause harm to a child.

Where a user is determined to be a "person with disability" under applicable law and has a lawful guardian, processing requires the guardian's consent. To register on behalf of such a user, please contact `privacy@undercity.online` before account creation.

## 8. Data Security and Breach Notification (Section 8(5))

We implement reasonable security safeguards including:
- TLS encryption in transit;
- Hashed authentication credentials (via Firebase Auth);
- Cryptographically hashed device fingerprints;
- Encrypted database backups;
- Rate limiting, anti-cheat detection, and audit logging;
- Strict role-based access controls.

In the event of a **personal data breach**, we will:
- **Notify the Data Protection Board of India** within 72 hours of becoming aware;
- **Notify affected users** without undue delay, via email and/or in-game notification;
- **Publish a public statement** describing the nature of the breach, likely consequences, and mitigation measures.
