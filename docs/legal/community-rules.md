# Undercity — Community Rules

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** [DATE]

> *The streets have rules. Break them, and you're done.*

These are the plain-English rules of the Undercity community. They exist alongside our [Terms of Service](./global-tos.md) and apply to **every player, every interaction, every time**.

If you're ever unsure whether something is allowed — **don't do it, and ask a moderator first**.

---

## 🔴 ZERO TOLERANCE — Instant Permanent Ban

These will get you and any associated accounts permanently banned. No warnings. No appeals on first offense.

### 1. Real-Life Threats or Doxxing
Threats of violence, threats to family, sharing or threatening to share anyone's real-world identity, address, phone number, workplace, school, photos, or other personal information.

### 2. Hate Speech
Slurs, harassment, or discrimination based on race, religion, caste, gender, sexual orientation, disability, nationality, or any other protected characteristic.

### 3. Child Safety Violations
Any content sexualizing or endangering minors. Any contact with a player you suspect is under 18. Report immediately to `support@undercity.online`.

### 4. Multi-Accounting
One account per real human. Our anti-cheat system (UAC) detects this and bans **all** linked accounts permanently.

### 5. Botting / Scripting
Automating any in-game action — crimes, gym, attacks, chat. Our behavior engine detects timing patterns; you will be caught.

### 6. Real-Money Trading (RMT)
Buying, selling, gifting, or trading in-game currency, items, or accounts for real money or anything outside the game. Both parties get banned.

### 7. Bug Exploitation for Gain
If you find a bug, **report it** to `security@undercity.online`. Exploiting it = ban + full rollback of any gains. Reporters may receive rewards.

### 8. Impersonating Staff
Pretending to be a developer, moderator, or administrator is an instant ban.

---

## 🟡 SERIOUS — Warning → Trust Score Drop → Ban

### 9. Harassment of Other Players
Persistent unwanted contact, insults, brigading, organized targeting. Disagreements happen — harassment is different.

### 10. Spamming
Repetitive messages in chat, forum, or private messages. Includes copy-paste flooding and self-promotion of unrelated services.

### 11. Scamming Outside Game Mechanics
Tricking other players into giving up items or currency through deception that's not part of the game's design. (PvP combat and in-game theft mechanics are allowed; tricking someone with a fake link is not.)

### 12. Sharing or Using Cheats
Posting hacks, exploits, third-party tools, or links to them. Even discussing where to get them.

### 13. Ban Evasion
Creating new accounts after being banned. All evasion accounts banned permanently; original ban extended.

### 14. NSFW / Explicit Content in Public Spaces
Sexual, gory, or disturbing content in usernames, forum posts, or public chat. We are an 18+ game but not a porn site.

### 15. Inciting Others to Break Rules
Encouraging other players to cheat, harass, multi-account, or violate any rule above.

---

## 🟢 MINOR — Warning → Mute / Cooldown

### 16. Off-Topic Chat in Wrong Channel
Use the right forum sections. Repeated wrong-channel posting may result in a mute.

### 17. Mild Toxicity in Heat of the Game
Some salty trash talk during PvP is expected. Persistent toxicity becomes harassment (see #9).

### 18. Reporting Players Falsely
Filing fake reports against other players to get them in trouble = warning, then ban for repeat offense.

## ⚖️ How Enforcement Works

### Trust Score System
Every player starts at **100 trust**. Violations reduce your score:

| Score    | Status        | What it means                                              |
|----------|---------------|------------------------------------------------------------|
| 70–100   | CLEAN         | Normal play, no restrictions                               |
| 40–69    | WATCHED       | Internal flag; you may not notice anything                 |
| 20–39    | SUSPICIOUS    | Lower trust; some actions may be reviewed                  |
| 1–19     | SHADOW BANNED | Silent penalties (reduced rewards, slower progression)     |
| 0        | HARD BANNED   | Account permanently locked; no access                      |

Trust recovery: clean play over time slowly restores your score. Reach out to `support@undercity.online` if you believe a flag was a mistake.

### Appeals
If you're banned and believe it was wrongful:
- Email `support@undercity.online` with subject line `Ban Appeal — [your username]`;
- Include any context you think we should know;
- We will respond within 7 days;
- **Arguing publicly in chat/forum/Discord will hurt, not help, your appeal.**

### Permanent Bans Are Permanent
We mean it. Don't make a new account. Don't ask friends to plead. Don't DM staff. Use the appeal email only.

## 🎮 PvP and Conflict — What's Allowed

The game is built around conflict. The following are **totally allowed** and part of the experience:

- Attacking other players (within mechanics);
- Stealing through in-game theft features;
- Betraying gang members;
- Forming alliances and breaking them;
- Trash talk that stays in-character and within reason;
- Strategic deception in trades/missions where the game supports it.

The line is between **in-game conflict** (allowed, fun, intended) and **real-life harm** (banned, never tolerated).

When in doubt: would this hurt the person *outside* the game? If yes → don't.

---

## 🛡️ How to Report Something

- **Player behavior** (harassment, cheating, scamming): in-game report button (coming soon) or email `support@undercity.online`;
- **Security vulnerability or bug**: email `security@undercity.online`;
- **Doxxing, threats, child safety**: email `support@undercity.online` with subject `URGENT` — we will respond within 24 hours;
- **DMCA / copyright**: email `legal@undercity.online`.

Always include:
- The username of the player involved;
- A screenshot or description of what happened;
- The approximate date and time;
- Any other relevant context.

False reports waste our time and are a violation in themselves (#18 above).

## 🤝 The Spirit of the Rules

We're not trying to babysit anyone. Undercity is a crime MMO and we expect rough edges. But there's a clear line between:

- A ruthless gangster character → ✅ great roleplay
- A real person sending real threats → ❌ instant ban

Play hard. Play smart. Play with respect for the person on the other side of the screen.

**Rise. Rule. Reign.** — but never at the cost of someone else's safety or dignity.
