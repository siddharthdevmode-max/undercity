# Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2025

## 1. Overview

This policy defines what is and is not acceptable behaviour
on Undercity. All users must comply with this policy.

## 2. Prohibited Activities

### 2.1 Cheating & Exploitation
- Using bots, scripts, or automation tools
- Exploiting game bugs or glitches for unfair advantage
- Using VPNs or proxies to evade bans or restrictions
- Creating multiple accounts (multi-accounting)
- Sharing accounts with other players
- Purchasing, selling, or trading accounts

### 2.2 Hacking & Security
- Attempting to access other users' accounts
- Attempting to bypass authentication or security systems
- Probing, scanning, or testing system vulnerabilities without permission
- Attempting SQL injection, XSS, or other attacks
- Interfering with game servers or infrastructure
- Reverse engineering game code or systems

### 2.3 Harmful Behaviour
- Harassment, bullying, or stalking other users
- Hate speech based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexuality, disability
- Threats of real-world violence
- Doxxing (publishing private information about others)
- Impersonating staff, moderators, or other players
- Spreading malware or malicious links

### 2.4 Content Violations
- Posting illegal content of any kind
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) — zero tolerance, reported to authorities
- Content that violates intellectual property rights
- Spam or unsolicited advertising

### 2.5 Economic Abuse
- Real money trading (RMT) of virtual currency or items
- Money laundering through in-game economy
- Exploiting other players through deception or fraud
- Chargebacks or payment fraud

## 3. Consequences

Violations may result in:
- Warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent ban
- IP ban
- Legal action (for serious violations)

Severity of consequences depends on:
- Nature and severity of violation
- Whether it was intentional
- History of previous violations
- Impact on other users or the game

## 4. Reporting Violations

To report a player or content:
Email: katanas.reaper@gmail.com
Subject: Player Report — [username]

Include:
- Reported player's username
- Description of the violation
- Evidence (screenshots if possible)
- Date and time of incident

## 5. Appeals

If you believe an action against your account was unjust:
Email: katanas.reaper@gmail.com
Subject: Account Appeal — [your username]

Include:
- Your username
- The action taken against your account
- Why you believe it was unjust
- Any supporting evidence

We will review appeals within 7 days.

## 6. Enforcement Discretion

We reserve the right to take action against any behaviour
we determine to be harmful to the community or game, even
if not explicitly listed in this policy.
