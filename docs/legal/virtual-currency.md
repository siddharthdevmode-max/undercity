# Virtual Currency Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2025

## 1. Overview

Undercity uses virtual currencies for gameplay purposes only.

## 2. Types of Virtual Currency

### 2.1 Cash (In-Game Money)
- Earned through crimes, jobs, and other game activities
- Used to purchase in-game items, properties, and services
- Can be lost through failed crimes, attacks, and other game events

### 2.2 Points
- Earned through special achievements and activities
- Used for premium in-game features
- Cannot be purchased with real money (at this time)

## 3. No Real-World Value

Virtual currencies in Undercity:
- Have **no real-world monetary value**
- **Cannot be exchanged** for real money or gift cards
- **Cannot be transferred** outside the game
- **Cannot be redeemed** for any real-world goods or services
- Are **not legal tender** in any jurisdiction

## 4. No Purchase with Real Money

Currently, Undercity's virtual currencies:
- **Cannot be purchased** with real money
- Are earned **exclusively through gameplay**

If we introduce real-money purchases in the future:
- We will update this policy
- We will provide clear pricing
- We will comply with applicable consumer protection laws

## 5. Real Money Trading (RMT) — Prohibited

The sale, purchase, or trade of virtual currency for real money is
**strictly prohibited** and will result in:
- Immediate permanent account ban
- Forfeiture of all virtual currency and items
- Potential legal action

This includes:
- Selling accounts for real money
- Trading in-game items for real money outside the game
- Any third-party marketplace transactions

## 6. Virtual Currency Loss

We are not responsible for virtual currency lost due to:
- Voluntary gameplay decisions
- Account bans for ToS violations
- Game rebalancing or wipes
- Account inactivity (if applicable)
- Hacking due to compromised personal credentials

## 7. No Inheritance or Transfer on Death

Virtual currency and items cannot be transferred to another person
in the event of account holder death or incapacity.

## 8. Account Termination

Upon account termination (voluntary or involuntary):
- All virtual currency is forfeited
- No compensation will be provided
- This applies regardless of how the currency was earned

## 9. Changes to Virtual Economy

We reserve the right to:
- Adjust virtual currency earn rates
- Change item prices
- Introduce or remove game features
- Conduct economy resets (with reasonable notice)
