# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2025
**Effective Date:** June 2025

## 1. Who We Are

Undercity ("we", "us", "our") is a text-based crime MMO browser game.
Contact: katanas.reaper@gmail.com

## 2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.
We also use similar technologies like localStorage and sessionStorage.

## 3. Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies
These cannot be disabled — the game cannot function without them.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Firebase Auth Token | Keeps you logged in | Session / 1 hour |
| Session ID | Identifies your session | Session |
| CSRF Token | Security protection | Session |

### 3.2 Functional Cookies
These remember your preferences.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| theme | Dark/Light mode preference | 1 year |
| cookie_consent | Stores your cookie choices | 1 year |
| age_verified | Stores age gate confirmation | 1 year |

### 3.3 Analytics Cookies (Opt-in Only)
We do NOT currently use analytics cookies.
If we add them in future, we will ask for your consent first.

### 3.4 Third-Party Cookies

| Provider | Purpose | Their Policy |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Firebase (Google) | Authentication | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| Cloudflare Turnstile | Bot protection / CAPTCHA | [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) |
| FingerprintJS | Anti-cheat device identification | [FingerprintJS Privacy Policy](https://fingerprint.com/privacy-policy/) |

## 4. How We Use Device Fingerprinting

We use FingerprintJS to generate a device fingerprint for **anti-cheat purposes only**.
This helps us detect:
- Multi-account abuse
- Bot activity
- VPN/proxy abuse

This data is **never sold** and **never used for advertising**.

## 5. Your Choices

### Manage Cookies
You can control cookies through:
- Our Cookie Consent Banner (shown on first visit)
- Your browser settings
- Clearing your browser data

### Browser Settings
- Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
- Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
- Safari: Preferences → Privacy
- Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

### Opt Out of Firebase
Visit [Google's safety centre](https://safety.google.com)

### Opt Out of Cloudflare
Visit [Cloudflare's privacy page](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

## 6. Essential Cookies Cannot Be Disabled

If you disable essential cookies (Firebase Auth), you will not be able to
log in or play the game. This is a technical requirement, not a choice.

## 7. Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals.
When DNT is enabled, we disable all non-essential tracking.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of significant changes via:
- In-game notification
- Email (if you have provided one)
